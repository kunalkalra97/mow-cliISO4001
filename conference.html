ICES

International Civil Engineering Symposium (ICES),is a yearly specialized fest by SCALE(School of Civil and Chemical Engineering) of the Vellore Institute of Technology. It is a symposium typically held amid the long stretch of March each year. ICES organized by ASCE-VIT (in a joint effort with the American Society of Civil Engineers India segment) of VIT University is one of the largest get-together of Civil Engineers from all of India and abroad.

ICES CONFERENCE

ASCE-VIT in collaboration with SCALE-VIT, conducts conference, which covered fields like: water treatment, hydrological processes in natural and urban environment, soil and ground water remediation, water reuse, contemporary water quality issues, hydrology in the field of water resources.

Environmental engineering forms an important field in civil, thus themes like alternative energy and their environmental impacts, environmental biotechnology, Biotransformation and fate of environmental contaminants, Air quality: air pollution, Energy and environmental impacts of infrastructure systems, Sustainable transportation planning, Sustainable environment, Reliability and risk analysis, pollutant transport and contaminant Transport Modelling were included.

An interesting and relatively new field, information technology in construction has also been included, thus encouraging progressive Civil stream positions itself on central subjects like structural, earthquake, and geotechnical and transport engineering, and hence included in the conference.
