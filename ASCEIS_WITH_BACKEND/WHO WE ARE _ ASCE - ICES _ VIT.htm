American Society of Civil Engineers (ASCE) is a Student Chapter in Vellore Institute of Technology, Vellore. It has been operating since past 6 years. ASCE has successfully been organising 4 International Civil Engineering Symposiums in VIT, Vellore. ICES which is organized by ASCE – student chapter of VIT University is one of the biggest gathering of Civil Engineers from all over India and abroad. The symposium is a platform for multiple events and workshops and an International Conference. ASCE also organises workshops and events which have been extremely popular and successful among the students
