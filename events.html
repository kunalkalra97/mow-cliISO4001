Surveyor Challenge

This competition is to test teams on basic surveying techniques such as distance measurements, levelling, and traversing.
The competition will consist one or all of the following events:

Distance Measurement: Each team will tape and pace several distances between fixed points.

Levelling: Each team will calculate the elevation of several turning points between one benchmark to another benchmark.

Traverse: Each team will find the interior angles of a traverse that is made by a series of fixed points.

Arch Bridge

The participants will be provided with bricks, sand and water along with a supporting arch. They would build a bridge over the arch without using any binding material cement within a given period of time after which the support is removed after which the arch is tested.

Traffineer 1.0

Teams will be provided with a transportation problem at the time of the challenge and asked to develop a solution. Each team will apply the principles of transportation planning, traffic operations, and highway design to respond to the design problem. The response will be primarily qualitative; thus teams should focus on development of an innovative conceptual design that would provide a long-term transportation solution. Teams will be granted with two hours to prepare a proposed conceptual solution, organize submittal materials, and prepare a 5-minute presentation. Team presentations will be delivered to a panel of judges for review, which could include a short question and answer period.
